Beauty

by k. a. calkins

It's no secret that much of the world is displeased with the way that America handles itself. And whether we like to believe it or not, 6 billion people can't really be wrong. If we, as Americans and individuals, were to work at being people that we could be proud of in the eyes of the rest of the world, I believe our lives and our planet would be that much better off. Even foreign policy comes down to the golden rule, so lets treat and respect the rest of the world like we'd like to be. (I realize i was not the first to use the double meaning: us/U.S. but it worked so well with this sign i had to borrow it, so thank you for the idea the other artists who used it)
