Nothing is FREE.

by S-Doody

I feel the ideas these candidates are selling are being eaten up by the American people without any research or rational thought. People think that if the government were to control all aspects of our lives, things will be better for everyone. They think socialized health care is the answer - but they don't look past the smiling candidates and catchy slogans to see the problems that these FREE things will cause us as a country. They think a windfall tax will save companies, when in reality - these taxes will only get passed onto you, the American consumer; but people are refusing to believe these terrible things. Let's not let a smiling candidate ruin what we have all worked so hard to build and keep alive. I strongly recommend research and rational thought. Question everything.
