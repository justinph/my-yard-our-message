Weapons of Mass Destruction

by mniss

26 people would put this sign in their yard.

Sign description:
This poster, first exhibited in the "Errata and Contradictions" visual poetry show at the Dudley House Gallery, Harvard University in 2004, shows the successive reworkings of Bush's justification for the war in Iraq. Each color represents a level of emendation of the original black text. "Iraq has Weapons of Mass Destruction."
