LockStep

by lisa link

6 people would put this sign in their yard.

Sign description:
I made the piece today first as a response to the way people follow the "party line" in elections. It also is a response to the way many media outlets will retell the same story (often nasty items about a candidate) over and over without questioning the source. It is a response to the corporate consolidation of media ownership and in general of the culture surrounding elections.
