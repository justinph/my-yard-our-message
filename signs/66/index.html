Vote and Live Well

by k. a. calkins

45 people would put this sign in their yard.

Sign description:
Voting is a great thing to do, but it doesn't stop there. This sign was intended to remind us that filling out the ballot isn't the end of our responsibility to this country or the rest of the world. We need to be good citizens and work at making this a better planet everyday. If we all did that, it would make a greater difference than any guy that gets elected President.
