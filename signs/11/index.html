No Vote? No Voice.

by S-Doody

25 people would put this sign in their yard.

Sign description:
When the topic of the presidential campaign comes up, I hear a number of people saying "I'm not going to vote this time." I see this as ignorance. Every legal person who has a right to vote should do so. If the American fail to vote on issues, then our Democracy will fall apart and who knows what may come of it. We need to SPEAK UP and EXERCISE our rights and freedoms as individuals. I feel the ideas these candidates are selling are being eaten up by the American people without any research or rational thought. I strongly recommend research and rational thought. Question everything.
