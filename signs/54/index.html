Vote Your Savior

by Bill Fisher

23 people would put this sign in their yard.

Sign description:
Yard Sign Project: Inspired by the events at Abu Ghraib and the endless War on Terror, these red, white and blue signs are designed to resemble the ubiquitous yard signs printed commercially and posted in front of homes and businesses throughout the southern USA. "Vote Your Savior" makes explicit the merging of secular and religious iconography implied by the rhetoric of our political leaders.
